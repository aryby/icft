Welcome to ICCIP 2025!

2025 The 11th International Conference on Communication and Information Processing (ICCIP 2025) will significantly contribute to address the unprecedented research advances in the areas of communication & information processing. It will be held in Lingshui, Hainan, China during November 12-15, 2025. It's sponsored by Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China; and hosted by BUPT_QMSH.

2025年第11届通信和信息处理国际会议将在海南·陵水举行， 会期是2025年11月12-15日。 本次活动由北京邮电大学主办。这次活动将是国际各领域专家齐聚一堂讨论本领域重大问题、经验交流讨论的好机会。 陵水，国内海岛天花板，媲美三亚的宝藏旅行地，是一座一年四季如春的滨海城市。在此我们向您发出诚挚的邀请! 这次活动将是国际各领域专家齐聚一堂讨论本领域重大问题、经验交流讨论的好机会。

ICCIP 2025 is dedicated to explore the cutting-edge applications of communication & information processing technologies to enhance the future of applications. ICCIP 2025's key theme on “communication and information processing”.

This conference will feature world-class plenary speakers, major technical symposiums, industry and academic panels, workshops, tutorials and invited tracks. The technical program chairs invite the submission of original papers for proceedings.This international conference serves a forum for researchers to address. The conference covers almost all the areas of this field.

我们将会举办信息技术相关的创新竞赛和研讨活动，期待本次活动能助力信息技术的发展，打造多方参与、协同高效的创新格局，优秀作品会收录在远程共享实践平台上并进行展示。
竞赛主题：信息技术应用系统的设计与实践，包括区块链、大数据、云计算、人工智能等领域，可结合医疗、 教育、能源等领域的信息技术服务场景，设计基于信息技术的应用系统；关键核心信息技术的研发，包括区块 链、大数据、云计算、人工智能等领域，对于现有的信息技术进行创新性研究和开发

Publication Support

Submitted paper will be peer reviewed by conference committees. The accepted paper will be included into ICCIP 2025 Conference Proceedings, which will be archived in the ACM Digital Library, and indexed by Ei Compendex and Scopus.

The ISBN number assigned to ICCIP 2025 is 979-8-4007-2192-2

所有文章将由程序委员会严格审核，所有录用文章在完成注册后被收录出版至 ICCIP 2025会议论文集， 并在会后被 EI Compendex 和 Scopus 检索，文章作者将被邀请到会参会展示研究报告。
